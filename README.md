# 🛒 Flipkart Black Journey

## Product Strategy Case Study

> **A product strategy case study focused on improving Flipkart's post–Big Billion Days customer retention through a personalized 60-day Flipkart Black Journey.**

---

# 📖 Overview

Big Billion Days (BBD) is Flipkart's flagship shopping festival that attracts millions of customers through deep discounts and exclusive launches. While the event drives massive customer acquisition, many shoppers become inactive immediately after the sale and return only during the next major sale event.

This project proposes **Flipkart Black Journey**, a personalized 60-day post-BBD retention program designed to convert sale-driven buyers into loyal customers by encouraging at least **one full-price purchase within 60 days**.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

**How might Flipkart encourage Big Billion Days buyers to return and make at least one full-price purchase within 60 days after the sale?**

### Objectives

- Increase post-BBD customer retention
- Encourage full-price purchases
- Improve customer engagement
- Increase Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Reduce dependence on seasonal sales

---

# 🔍 Research Methodology

The solution was developed using a combination of primary and secondary research.

### Primary Research

- Conducted a Google Forms survey with **50+ respondents**
- Conducted **10 one-to-one user interviews**
- Created user personas based on research insights
- Mapped the complete customer journey to identify retention gaps

### Secondary Research

- Reddit discussions
- Google Play Store reviews
- Industry reports
- News articles
- E-commerce market research

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

## Flipkart Black Journey

A personalized 60-day retention experience that begins immediately after every Big Billion Days purchase.

### Key Features

- 🎁 Flipkart Black Starter Pass
- 🤖 AI-powered personalized recommendations
- 🎯 Milestone-based shopping missions
- 🔔 Smart reminders & engagement nudges
- ⭐ Priority customer support
- 🛍️ Cross-category exploration
- 🏆 Reward-based progression
